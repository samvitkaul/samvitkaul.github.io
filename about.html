To build a scientific network and an online platform of the Kashmiri pandit researchers so at to facilitate connections, research collaborations as well as build & nurture NextGen scientific talent.

Promotion of Scientific Careers & STEM for Next generation community talent

What is the purpose of KSC? What will drive us in 2021-2022 timeframe?

  • KP global scientist's data base – building the KSC platform
  • Hold seminars Seminars – Keynotes - Stories
  • Mentorship programs – Nurture and Support
  • Lab and thought collaborations across all levels
  • Kashmiri young scientist talent hunt – - A global Quick-fire Challenge

Key Aim and Deliverables – 4 Goals

  1. Goal 1: Create relevant database of KP scientists and researchers
    • This platform can be a website and hosting all relevant data
    • A web form and page is being created
  2. Goal 2: Holding Keynote seminars and Scientific Discussions
    • Organize Quarterly Keynote seminars – all talks virtual at the moment
    • Aim to link each talk to Core central theme of philosophy of life and its processes
    • Connecting various scientific disciplines with the underlying themes
    • Connecting with Kashmiri history, Indian history and ancient and modern scientific ethos
  3. Goal 3: A global Quick-fire Challenge – a science talent hunt
    A Quick-fire Challenge
    • Kashmiri young scientist talent search
    • Age groups – 9-12, 12-16 and above 16-21
    • Theme – scientific innovation challenge
    • Prize money (donations and contributions)
    • External jury and collaboration
  4. Goal 4: Build mentorship Program & Social Impact programs?
    • how to help, build the trust and support the next generation scientists?
    • how to build scientific role models for the community?
    • How to promote science and curiosity?
    • How to drive a social impact program
